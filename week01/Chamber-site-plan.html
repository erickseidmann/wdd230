Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Campinas Business Association

The site name for this chamber of commerce website iis "Campinas Chamber of Commerce." This name was selected to reflect the locality and purpose of the website, which is to promote businesses and community development in the Campinas.

Site Purpose

The primary purposes of our website is:

  1. To serve as a platform for local businesses to promote their products and services.
  2. To provide information about upcoming eventes, networking opportunities, and resources for businesses in Campinas.

Scenarios

Here are some scenarios that the website will address for the audience:

  • "What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?"
  • "Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?"
  • "What has been the population growth in Campinas?"

Color Schema

The color scheme for this website will reflect the spirit of Campinas and the chamber of commerce. I will use the following colors:

  • Primary Color: #79abe0
  • Secondary Color: #f0f0f0

Typography

I will use the following fonts for this website:

  • sans-serif - Headings
  • Slab Serif - Body text

These fonts will maintain consistency and readability across the website.
